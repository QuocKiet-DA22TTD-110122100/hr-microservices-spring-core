# Product

## Register

product

## Users

HR administrators, payroll operators, managers, department heads, and employees use this system during daily HR work. They are usually checking permissions, reviewing people data, approving work, and moving between microservice-backed modules.

## Product Purpose

The product is an internal HR workspace that brings authentication, role-aware navigation, employee records, projects, tasks, organization data, and payroll workflows into one React interface backed by Spring Boot services. Success means users can identify their role, find the right module quickly, and trust that restricted actions are clearly scoped.

## Brand Personality

Calm, operational, trustworthy. The interface should feel like a focused workbench for HR operations, not a marketing site.

## Anti-references

Avoid decorative SaaS landing-page patterns, heavy AI-purple gradients, generic glass cards, oversized hero sections, and visual noise that makes routine HR work feel theatrical.

## Design Principles

- Make role and permission context visible without making it loud.
- Prefer dense, scannable layouts over decorative sections.
- Use restrained color for navigation, state, and priority only.
- Keep standard controls familiar and consistent across modules.
- Design empty, loading, warning, and error states as part of the workflow.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast for text and controls. Preserve keyboard focus states, reduce motion for users who request it, and avoid relying on color alone for status or priority.
