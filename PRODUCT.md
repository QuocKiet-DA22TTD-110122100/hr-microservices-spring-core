# Product

## Register

product

## Users

HR staff, managers, department heads, employees, and administrators use this internal web system during daily work to manage people, projects, tasks, permissions, approvals, and payroll-adjacent HR records.

## Product Purpose

The product supports team work management and HR operations through a role-aware React workspace backed by Spring Boot microservices. Success means users can find the right operational list quickly, understand what needs attention, and complete updates before they block payroll, approvals, or reporting.

## Brand Personality

Clear, dependable, focused. The UI should feel like an internal operations tool that helps users act with confidence.

## Anti-references

Avoid marketing-style landing pages inside authenticated workspaces, decorative dashboards that hide tables, oversized hero sections, and vague status copy that does not tell HR what to do next.

## Design Principles

- Put urgent work first, especially records that block payroll or approvals.
- Make status, owner, due date, and next action visible without extra navigation.
- Keep role-specific workspaces dense enough for repeated operational use.
- Prefer inline detail and quick actions over disruptive modal flows.
- Preserve audit-friendly labels and data states.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target readable contrast, keyboard-accessible controls, clear disabled states, and reduced reliance on color alone for priority or status.
